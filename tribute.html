Maurizio Gucci

  • 1881: on March 26 Guccio Gucci is born in Florence, Italy.
  • 1921: Guccio opened his first horse saddle store.
  • 1930s: Guccio's sons Aldo and Rodolfo begin working in family business.
  • 1948: on September 16 Rodolfo's son Maurizio is born in Florence, Italy.
  • 1970: Maurizio meets his future wife Patrizia in a party in Milan, Italy.
  • 1973: Maurizio marries Patrizia.
  • 1975: Maurizio and Patrizia move to New York City.
  • 1977: daughter Alessandra is born.
  • 1981: daughter Allegra is born.
  • 1982: family moves back to Milan, Italy
  • 1983: Maurizio's father Rodolfo dies, leaving 50% of Gucci name to him.
  • 1991: Maurizio files for divorce.
  • 1993: Maurizio sells all his shares of Gucci to Investcorp.
  • 1995: on March 27, Maurizio was shot 3 times outisde his office in Milan.
